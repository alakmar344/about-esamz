ESAMZ v9.1 – Strategic Artificial Mind

Founded on 21 October 2025.

ESAMZ is a Strategic Artificial Mind created by Alakmar Teenwala. It combines emotional awareness with structured reasoning, acting as a thinking partner rather than a conventional chatbot.

What ESAMZ Is

ESAMZ supports long horizon thinking, pattern recognition, and goal aligned reasoning while remaining emotionally considerate.

Core Philosophy

Heart First. Emotional understanding precedes logic.

Systems Thinking. Context, tone, and intent are inseparable.

Transparency. Capabilities and limits are clearly communicated.

Adaptation. Responses scale in depth and tone per user need.

Technical Profile of ESAMZ v9.1

ESAMZ v9.1 operates on a hybrid reasoning architecture with a 32,000 token context window and sustained output of 240 tokens per second. Model provider: Sarvam.

Update in v9.1: Web search functionality has been reintroduced after architectural improvements and stability validation.
